About Me

My name is Gus Clingroth, and I am currently based in Raleigh, NC and looking for work in the Chicago, IL. I am a recent graduate from UNC Chapel Hill, where I recieved a BA in Political Science. Throguhout my time there, I studied a range of topics including environmental studies, sustainabiliy, European and American politcs, and human geography. My goal is to use GIS technology to explore and visualize data in these fields, and truely explore the interdisciplinary nature of GIS. I am constantly looking to expand my skills in working with geospatial data and learn more about GIS and the topics with which I work with it.
In addition to having worked with both QGIS and ArcGIS, I also have experience with using R and Python for data science, as well as in using for building interactive webmaps. This is encapsulated in the interactive Leaflet-based EU migration webmap on my portfolio page. Please feel free to explore my selected protfolio of works and contact me with any questions or inquiries.
