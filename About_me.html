About Me

  I am currently based in Raleigh, NC and looking for work in the Chicago, IL. I am a recent graduate from UNC Chapel Hill, where I received a BA in Political Science. Throughout my time there, I studied a range of topics including environmental studies, sustainability, and European and American politics. My goal is to use GIS technology to explore and visualize data in these fields. I am constantly looking for ways to expand my skills in working with geospatial data.

  In addition to having worked with both QGIS and ArcGIS, I have experience with several programming languages. I have experience with both both R and Python and have used JavaScript for building interactive web maps. Please feel free to explore my portfolio of selected works and contact me with any questions or inquiries.
